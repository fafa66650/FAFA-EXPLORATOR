❓ Help Center – FAFA Explorator

🧭 What is FAFA Explorator?

An immersive and fun treasure hunt across Banyuls-sur-Mer! Explore the secrets of the town through themed puzzles, whether you're solo, with family or in a group.

🎯 How to play?

  • 📍 Start at the meeting point and follow the clues.
  • 🔓 Enter codes to unlock the next challenge.
  • 🧠 Use the compass to reach each real location in town.

💡 Tips

If you're stuck, look carefully at the photos and text. Some puzzles may require logic, observation, or knowledge about Banyuls!

🏆 Bonus Puzzle

At the end of your adventure, enter the secret FAFA code to unlock a hidden puzzle and earn a special success!

📬 Contact

If you encounter any problem, contact us via fafa.explorator@gmail.com.
